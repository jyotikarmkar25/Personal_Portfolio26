Put your photo file in this folder.

How to add your photo:
1. Copy your photo from your laptop into this folder (public/photo/).
2. Name the file: my-photo.jpg  (or .png / .webp)
3. The website will automatically show it in the About section.

If you use a different filename or extension, update the src in index.html:
    <img src="/photo/my-photo.jpg" alt="Jyoti Karmkar">

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
Recommended: square image, at least 500x500 pixels.
